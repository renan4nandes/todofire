# Meet TodoFire
A new web app and a new easy way to manage your todos.

## What is TodoFire?
Is a web app to create, manage and delete your todos in a easy and intuitive way.

## How does it work?
Is easy! Just login with your Google account and start creating and deleting your todos!

## Creating todos
